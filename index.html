About Us

About Sufi Tecno Global Sdn. Bhd.

Sufi Tecno Global Sdn. Bhd. is an ICT and smart office solutions provider based in Kuantan, Pahang. We specialize in delivering reliable, scalable and cost-effective technology solutions tailored to modern business needs.
